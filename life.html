🔬 Biological Grid Physics

This simulation maps the mathematical concept of Emergence. Cells do not operate under centralized instructions; instead, macro-level life cycles develop spontaneously out of three basic localized laws tracking neighborhood density mass:

  • Underpopulation: Active cells with fewer than 2 neighbors instantly wither from isolation.
  • Overpopulation: Nodes with more than 4 neighbors suffocate from grid overcrowding.
  • Spontaneous Birth: An empty coordinate space ($0$) with exactly 3 or 4 neighbors forms a perfect vacuum equilibrium, birthing a fresh variable signature out of the absolute void.

🛑 Chronological Freezing Points

Is your grid halting, locking up, or stabilizing into unchanging architectures? This is completely expected behavior. Every chaotic ecosystem matrix ultimately cools into one of three cosmic mathematical destinies:

1. Static Equilibrium (Still Lifes)

Square crystalline clusters and cross blocks freeze perfectly solid. Every structural node in these formations has precisely 2 or 3 neighbors, shielding them from death permanently.

2. Perpetual Oscillators (Blinkers)

Clusters form looping, pulsing patterns. They switch from horizontal to vertical states endlessly, trapped in an infinite generation engine cycle ($A \rightarrow B \rightarrow A$).

3. Total Cosmic Devastation

Polarized fields expand unsustainably, triggering cascade crashes that clear the field back to absolute Day 0 nothingness.
